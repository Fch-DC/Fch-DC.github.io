Mythology

The word mythology comes from the word myth. A myth is a legend that people tell that gets passed down from generation to generation. Mythology is a group of myths that certain people told. Greek mythology started in Greece. Roman mythology started in Rome. Egyptian mythology started in Egypt. Here there is a page about Greek mythology, Roman mythology, and Egyptian mythology. In most myths there are Gods, monsters, Magic, and demigods/heros. That's what makes them fun and interesting, so I hope you enjoy mythology.
